About me

I'm Shaleen and I'm a

Shaleen Munuhe is a renewable energy, energy efficiency, policy and advocacy expert. She is currently working as an independent consultant providing business advisory to off grid companies in Kenya and also as the East Africa Programme Consultant for Ashden Award Program. Prior to that, she was the assistant Country Manager, Kenya, Somalia and Ghana at Tetra Tech International Development for the Africa Clean Energy Technical Assistance Facility, a 4-year programme funded by the UK Government Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO), aimed at catalyzing a market based approach for private sector delivery of renewable energy electrification technologies, with a focus on high quality stand-alone solar systems. She has a vast professional experience in the private and public sector. She provides technical support in matters relating to advocacy, project design to renewable energy stakeholders and companies. My work involves the development of feasibility studies, technical analysis, planning and design of projects, particularly in the stand-alone solar sub sector. She has conducted over twenty stakeholder workshops in the renewable energy field for multiple audiences including off grid companies, private sector, government officials and research and testing centers officials. She has extensive knowledge in utilizing renewable energy technologies for climate change adaptation and mitigation for economic growth in developing countries.
